Where is God when I'm hurting?

This is less an argument to win than a wound to sit with, so let me be gentle. Christianity's distinct answer to pain isn't a clever explanation - it's a God who didn't stay at a safe distance from it. In Jesus, God entered the world of grief, betrayal, torture, and death himself. Whatever you're facing, you're not facing it watched by a stranger.

Scripture says "the Lord is close to the brokenhearted" (Psalm 34:18), and that nothing - not trouble or anguish - can separate you from his love (Romans 8:38-39). Doubt and lament are not faithlessness; the Bible is full of both. If you're in a dark place right now, that's exactly where this God has promised to meet people.
