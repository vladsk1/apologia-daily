Answers to tough questions

Honest, evidence-based answers to the questions skeptics and seekers actually ask — about God, Jesus, the Bible, suffering, and faith. Short enough to read in a minute; each links to a deeper dive.

God's Existence
  • Is there any evidence that God exists?
  • What caused the universe?
  • Why is there something rather than nothing?
  • Is the universe fine-tuned for life?
  • Where do morality and human rights come from without God?
  • Can you be good without believing in God?
  • Doesn't science disprove God?
The Resurrection
  • Did Jesus really exist?
  • Is there evidence that Jesus rose from the dead?
  • Wasn't the resurrection just a legend that grew over time?
  • Why does it matter that women were the first witnesses to the empty tomb?
  • Could the disciples have stolen Jesus' body?
  • Did the disciples just hallucinate the risen Jesus?
  • Why did Paul go from persecuting Christians to dying for Jesus?
Bible Reliability
  • Has the Bible been changed over the centuries?
  • Can we trust the New Testament manuscripts?
  • Aren't there contradictions in the Bible?
  • Were the Gospels written by eyewitnesses?
  • Is there archaeological evidence for the Bible?
Who Jesus Is
  • Did Jesus actually claim to be God?
  • Isn't the Trinity a contradiction?
  • Wasn't Jesus just a good moral teacher?
  • Did the church invent Jesus' divinity at the Council of Nicaea?
  • Did Jesus fulfill Old Testament prophecy?
Suffering & Evil
  • If God is good, why is there so much suffering?
  • How can a loving God send people to hell?
  • Could evil actually be evidence FOR God?
  • Where is God when I'm hurting?
Faith & Doubt
  • Aren't all religions basically the same?
  • Isn't faith just believing without evidence?
  • Is it okay to have doubts as a Christian?
