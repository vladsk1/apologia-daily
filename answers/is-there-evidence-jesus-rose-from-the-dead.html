Is there evidence that Jesus rose from the dead?

Yes - and the case rests on facts accepted by most historians, not just believers. Jesus was crucified and buried. His tomb was found empty. Many different people, in different settings, sincerely reported seeing him alive afterwards. And former sceptics like Paul and James were transformed into bold witnesses willing to die.

The debate among historians isn't usually about these facts; it's about what explains them. Naturalistic theories - hallucination, theft, legend - each struggle to account for all of them at once. The resurrection explains the whole set simply and directly. "He is not here; he has risen" (Matthew 28:6).
