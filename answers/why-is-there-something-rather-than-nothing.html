Why is there something rather than nothing?

Everything in the universe exists - but nothing about it had to. It could all have failed to exist; it is "contingent." A contingent thing needs an explanation outside itself for why it exists rather than not.

And you can't explain the whole collection of contingent things by appealing to still more contingent things - that only pushes the question back a step. The chain has to rest on something that exists by its own nature and cannot fail to exist: a necessary being. That isn't just one more item in the universe; it is the reason there is a universe at all. Christians recognise that being as the God who said, "I AM WHO I AM" (Exodus 3:14).
