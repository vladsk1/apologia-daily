The Atheist
Philosophical · Science-based
Challenges your belief in God with logic, science, and the problem of evil. Draws on Dawkins, Hitchens, Harris, and Russell.
M
The Muslim
Theological · Scripture-based
Challenges the Trinity, the divinity of Jesus, and the reliability of the Bible. Draws on the Quran and Islamic scholarship.
?

Sceptical · Open-minded
Not hostile — genuinely uncertain. Asks sincere questions about evidence, suffering, and why Christianity over other religions.

Moral · Cultural
Argues religion causes harm, morality doesn't need God, and Christianity is exclusivist. Draws on humanist and social justice frameworks.

Choose a scenario

These aren't debates — they're real conversations. The AI plays a real person in your life. No scoring, no pressure. Just practise being present, honest, and gracious.

👔
The Curious Coworker
Friendly · Sceptical
A colleague who's heard you're a Christian and genuinely wants to understand why. Not hostile — just curious and a little sceptical.
👨‍👩‍👧
The Sceptical Family Member
Familiar · Challenging
A sibling or parent who thinks faith is a crutch. They love you but think you've been fooled. Conversations happen at the dinner table.
💔
The Grieving Friend
Emotional · Searching
A close friend who has just suffered a loss and is asking "where was God?" This requires pastoral sensitivity, not arguments.
🎓
The University Student
Intellectual · Newly Sceptical
A friend who's just started university and has been told by lecturers that science has disproved God. Enthusiastic but not deeply informed.
🧑
Your Teenager
Personal · Doubting
Your child has started asking hard questions — "Is any of this actually true?" This is one of the most important conversations you'll ever have.
🏡
The Open Neighbour
Spiritual · Exploring
A neighbour who's been through a hard time and seems spiritually open. They've asked you what you believe. This is a door God may be opening.
