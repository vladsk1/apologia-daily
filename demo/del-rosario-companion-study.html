Featured club
Can we show — by the standards of professional historians — that Jesus claimed to be God? Dr. Mikel Del Rosario builds the case through two parallel “blasphemy accusation” scenes: the paralytic Jesus forgives in Mark 2 (authority to forgive sins), and the Jewish examination in Mark 14 (authority to judge). Each scene runs the same three beats — the core scene, the blasphemy charge, the divine claim. This club walks the book chapter by chapter, then hands you the tools to defend it.

How the club works

Read Dr. Del Rosario's book chapter by chapter, with discussion questions for each — that's the whole club, and the book is all you need. Then, if you want to go further, each session links a few optional Apologia Daily resources to practise defending the case (Ask Anything, the Debate Arena, a game). They're clearly marked as ours, and entirely skippable.
