Featured club
Can we show — by the standards of professional historians — that Jesus claimed to be God? Del Rosario builds the case through two Gospel scenes: the paralytic Jesus forgives in Mark 2, and the trial where he answers the high priest in Mark 14. This club walks that argument one session at a time, then hands you the tools to defend it.

What makes this club different

A normal reading club stops at discussion questions. This one connects each chapter to Apologia Daily's certified evidence pages and a live drill — answer the objection in Ask Anything, spar it in the Debate Arena, catch it in a game. You don't just read the argument; you rehearse saying it.
